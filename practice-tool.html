Clinical Formulation

Your reading of the intake material through the model. This is your clinical thinking — not a summary of what the person said.

Developmental Reading
Which developmental layer is most live? Pre-symbolic (basic fault)? True/false self? Separation-individuation? Mentalization failure? More than one may be present — name the primary level and any secondary.
Be specific. Generic warmth does not repair a specific wound. The reparation must match the developmental level.
This is not pathology. It is the logical adaptation to what was available. Name it with respect.
Relational Pattern RIG / CCRT
In Stern's terms: what does this person expect to happen when they bring the full weight of something to another person? This is the destructive RIG that the therapeutic relationship must provide a new version of.
Generic warmth does not repair a specific wound. Name the specific relational provision required.
Attachment & Mentalization
Secure / anxious-preoccupied / dismissive-avoidant / disorganised. Note what in the narrative style, not just the content, informs this reading.
Can this person hold their own and another's mind simultaneously? Under what conditions does mentalization collapse? What does epistemic trust look like here?
Fonagy: when mirroring reflects the caregiver's state rather than the infant's, something foreign is internalised. What is this person externalising — and into whom?
Nervous System & Window
Porges / Van der Kolk: how wide is the window? What narrows it? What system — ventral vagal, sympathetic, dorsal vagal — is most active? This shapes the sequencing.
What does this person's body do in the room? What does your body do in response? Ogden: somatic countertransference as data.
Sequencing Position Seven Layers
Rogers (ground) → Kohut (container) → Stern (mechanism) → Levine (interrupted completion) → Van der Kolk (window) → Freud (descent) → Adler (reorientation). Name the current layer and what must be in place before progression becomes appropriate.
Working alliance / Transferential / Reparative / Person-to-person / Transpersonal. What is most active now and what does the clinical moment require?
Transferential & Intersubjective Field
What role is this person recruiting you into (Sandler's role responsiveness)? Is there a shared fantasy beginning to form? What organising principles from your own history are being activated?
Your somatic response. Your emotional response. What pulls you toward technique or distance. What makes you want to rescue, withdraw, or over-explain. This is clinical data.
Temporal Dimension Tonkin / Kübler-Ross
Kübler-Ross for the immediate: receive what is present, don't rush the stages. What needs to be held before anything else can be introduced?
The grief doesn't shrink — the person grows around it. What does a larger life look like for this person? Not as a goal to be stated, but as a clinical orientation.
Clinical Plan
Set 3 active here. Risk, competence, dual relationship, scope. Name honestly.
Session Update

Has the layer shifted? Is the window wider or narrower than last time? What does the nervous system appear to be doing with what has been offered?
Not a plan — an orientation. What must be available in you for the next session to be what this person needs?
