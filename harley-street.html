The setting

Upstairs, away from the street.

My London sessions take place at 117 Harley Street — upstairs, away from the noise of the street, in a quiet and private space in one of London’s most established addresses for health and wellbeing.

The room is calm and contained. Arriving here feels different from a busy clinic or a corporate office — it’s a space designed for the kind of conversation that needs privacy and unhurried attention.

Many of my London clients are navigating the pressures that come with demanding professional lives — work stress, leadership responsibility, burnout, or the particular difficulty of being expected to cope when privately struggling. Others come with relationship difficulties, anxiety, or a sense that something keeps happening that they haven’t been able to shift alone.

I am registered with Bupa and see a number of clients through private health insurance.
