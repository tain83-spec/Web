The setting

A genuinely welcoming atmosphere.

My Norwich sessions take place at 56A Thorpe Road — a community wellbeing centre with a genuinely welcoming atmosphere. There’s parking on site, coffee available, and a sense of warmth that feels quite different from a clinical setting.

Workshops and community events run from the building, which gives it an accessible, everyday feel — which many people find makes taking that first step a little less daunting.

I work with people from Norwich and the surrounding area experiencing a wide range of difficulties — anxiety, relationship struggles, people-pleasing, low mood, the impact of earlier experiences, and more. Some have tried therapy before. Many are coming for the first time.

Whatever brings you, you’ll find a consistent, confidential space to talk openly and begin to make sense of what’s going on.
